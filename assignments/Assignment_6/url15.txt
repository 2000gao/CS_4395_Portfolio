Dallas County is a county located in the U.S. state of Texas.
As of the 2010 census, the population was 2,368,139.
It is the second-most populous county in Texas and the ninth-most populous in the United States.
Its county seat is Dallas, which is also the third-largest city in Texas and the ninth-largest city in the United States.
